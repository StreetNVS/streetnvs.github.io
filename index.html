StreetNVS: Multi-Source Geometry Guidance
for Street-View Novel View Generation

Reconstruction-based methods interpolate well between observed views but fail when target poses depart sharply from the vehicle trajectory; purely generative methods produce plausible appearance without geometric consistency. We bridge the two by using LiDAR-grounded 3D anchors and relative camera poses to condition a controllable video generation backbone, preserving identity and structure under extreme extrapolation.

§1 · Unseen novel view synthesis

Synthesizing trajectories far from the vehicle path

For each scene we render four camera trajectories that depart from the original ego-trajectory: Elevation (BEV-like), Lane Shift (sideways translation), Spiral (orbital sweep around the source pose), and Rotation (yaw turn).
